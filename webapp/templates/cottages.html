Our Cabins

All cabins have been meticulously maintained over the years and offer the comfort and capacity to host small family vacations or entire family reunions. The cabins are nestled under mature pine and maple trees providing ample shade for those warm summer days. The vast majority of our beds have been upgraded from double to new queen beds this year. We offer a snack bar (Ollies Snack Shack) where you can buy a variety of items from ice cream to firewood. All larger cabins have their own bathroom and shower facilities, however, there is also a common bathhouse for the cabins without these facilities. All cabins are within one hundred yards of the beach and include a small deck and fire-pit for campfires.

Please note: Our minimum rental period in non-prime season is two days. Our minimum rental period in prime season is 7 days. Depending on size, facilities and the season, weekly cabin prices range from $360 - $1600 per week. BBQ rental and water cooler refills also available.
